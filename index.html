Short Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Zhejiang University supervised by Prof. Guofeng Zhang. Before that, I have received the B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from the Harbin Engineering University in 2016.
My research interests include 3D reconstruction, SLAM and Augmented Reality.
